How to export your LinkedIn connections

Follow these steps to get your Connections.csv file from LinkedIn:

  1. Go to linkedin.com and click your profile picture → Settings & Privacy
  2. Click Data Privacy in the left sidebar
  3. Under "How LinkedIn uses your data," click Get a copy of your data
  4. Select Connections and click Request archive
  5. LinkedIn will email you a download link — usually within 10 minutes
  6. Download and unzip the file, then upload Connections.csv here
