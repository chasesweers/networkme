How to export your LinkedIn connections

Follow these steps to get your Connections.csv file from LinkedIn:

  1. Go to linkedin.com → click your profile picture → Settings & Privacy
  2. Click Data Privacy in the left sidebar
  3. Click Download my data
  4. Select the first option (Download larger data archive — this includes connections)
  5. Click Request archive — LinkedIn will email you a download link
  6. Download and unzip the file, then upload Connections.csv here
